Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the whole process take?

From placing your order to installation, typically 12-18 weeks. The main variable is permit approval time, which varies by cemetery. We'll give you a realistic timeline specific to your cemetery when you enquire.

What deposit do you require?

We ask for a 50% deposit plus the permit fee to get started. The remaining balance is due once your memorial is complete and ready for installation. We accept bank transfer, card payments, and can arrange payment plans if needed.

Do I need a permit for a memorial?

Yes, all cemeteries require a permit before any memorial can be installed. Don't worry—we handle the entire application process for you. The permit fee is usually £300-£500 depending on the cemetery.

What if my cemetery has specific rules?

Every cemetery has different regulations about memorial sizes, styles, and materials. We maintain a database of requirements for hundreds of cemeteries and will advise you on what's permitted before you commit.

Can I be present at the installation?

Absolutely. Many families choose to be present when the memorial is installed. Just let us know and we'll arrange a time that works for you. If you can't attend, we'll send you photos of the completed installation.

How do I care for a granite memorial?

Granite is very low maintenance. We recommend cleaning with plain water and a soft cloth once or twice a year. Avoid harsh chemicals or pressure washers. We provide a full aftercare guide with every memorial.
