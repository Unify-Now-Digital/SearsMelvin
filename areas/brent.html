Memorial Masons in Brent — Headstones, Gravestones & Permits

Cemetery-specific advice for Brent families, with permits handled end-to-end

Sears Melvin is a memorial mason serving families across Brent and the surrounding North-West London area. We design and install headstones, gravestones and kerb sets at every cemetery in the borough — and we handle the cemetery permit application for you. Brent operates several council cemeteries with consistent rules across sites, which makes the borough one of the more predictable to plan a memorial in.

Cemeteries we work with in Brent

The cemeteries serving Brent families:

  • Carpenders Park Lawn Cemetery — Operated by Brent Council in Hertfordshire — designated lawn section with material restrictions.
  • Paddington Old Cemetery — Mill Hill Lane, Kilburn — Victorian cemetery with traditional and lawn areas.
  • Willesden New Cemetery — Long-established Brent cemetery with mixed sections.
  • Alperton Cemetery — Smaller Brent Council cemetery with consistent rules across plots.

How permits work in Brent

Brent Council operates a centralised permit system across its cemeteries, with a typical turnaround of three to five weeks — among the faster permit timelines in London. Carpenders Park is a designated lawn section and the rules on stone size, polish and finish are stricter than at the council's other sites; the standard application form does not capture those restrictions, so it pays to use the lawn-specific form first time.

You can also use our London cemetery permit checker to look up rules and timescales for any borough.

Our process for Brent families

  1. Free design consultation and quote — usually within one working day of your enquiry.
  2. Design proof sent for approval, including layout, lettering and inscription wording.
  3. We submit the cemetery permit application to the correct operator on your behalf.
  4. Memorial is crafted in our workshop once the permit is approved.
  5. Installation arranged with the cemetery, typically within two weeks of permit approval.

Designs available

We craft memorials in granite, marble and slate, in upright headstone, lawn, kerb-set and cremation-tablet formats. All designs are BS 8415 compliant and aligned with Brent's cemetery regulations. Browse the full memorial range or compare materials in our care guide.
