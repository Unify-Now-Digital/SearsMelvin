Understanding Cemetery Permits

What is a permit?

A memorial permit gives you permission to install a headstone or kerb memorial on a grave. It's required by all cemeteries before any work can begin.

How long does it take?

Processing times vary by cemetery. Council-run cemeteries typically take 8-16 weeks. Church cemeteries may require additional faculty approval taking longer.

What about fees?

Permit fees range from £200 to £600 depending on the cemetery and memorial type. We include permit handling in our service and pay fees on your behalf.
